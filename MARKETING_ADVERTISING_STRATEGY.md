- [ ] Create thumbnail for video
- [ ] Write full description (500 chars) with pain point + solution
- [ ] Prepare 3 questions to answer (PH users will ask)

#### Launch Day (Monday, 12:01 AM PT)

## 💡 Marketing Hacks & Tactics

### 1. Referral Loop Magic
When users reach Level 5 or 7-day streak, show:
```
"Share your achievement!

Post to TikTok/Instagram and get +50 XP

or

Refer a friend and get +100 XP (unlimited)

[Share to TikTok] [Share to Instagram] [Referral Link]
```

This turns your users into marketers.

### 2. FOMO Mechanics
- Limited spots: "Only 100 free premium slots left!"
- Time pressure: "Offer ends Friday!"
- Social proof: "1,234 students already downloading"
- Scarcity: "Referral bonuses (while supplies last)"

### 3. Strategic Free Premium
Give free premium to:
- First 100 users (week 1)
- Top 10% referrers (ongoing)
- Early reviewers who give 4+ stars
- Influencers/ambassadors

This creates loyal power users who evangelize.

### 4. Cross-Platform Promotion
- TikTok → Link to YouTube playlist
- YouTube → Link to email signup
- Email → Referral to TikTok/Instagram
- Referral → Premium trial → YouTube channel

Create a content funnel.

### 5. Timing Optimization
Best times to post/run ads:
- **Sunday-Thursday 6-10 PM** (students winding down)
- **Weekday mornings 7-9 AM** (college students)
- Avoid: Friday night → Tuesday morning (students busy/traveling)

### 6. Message Variation Testing
Test 3 different messages and see which converts best:
- **Student-focused:** "Never fall behind in class again"
- **Grade-focused:** "Go from C to A+ instantly"
- **Time-focused:** "Save 10 hours/week on notes"
- **Fun-focused:** "Get rewarded for studying"

Winner becomes main message.

### 7. Review Asking
After first week of usage:
```
"Loving Koala.ai? Help us grow!

⭐ Leave a quick review on the [App Store/Play Store]"
```

Great reviews = higher app store rankings = organic discovery

- ❌ Forget mobile optimization
