## Video Production (Simple Setup)

### Equipment Needed
- Your computer (already have it)
- Screen recording software (OBS Studio - FREE)
- Microphone (phone microphone OK, or $30 USB mic)
- That's it.

### Workflow
1. **Script** (5-10 minutes writing)
2. **Screen record** (5-15 minutes recording)
3. **Edit** (DaVinci Resolve - FREE, or CapCut)
4. **Add captions** (auto-captions then review)
5. **Upload & optimize**

### Editing Checklist
- [ ] Add intro (Koala logo, 2-3 seconds)
- [ ] Add captions/subtitles (YouTube auto-generates, you review)
- [ ] Add background music (royalty-free YouTube library)
- [ ] Add lower third graphics (title/channel name)
- [ ] Add outro (Subscribe button, next video suggestion)
- [ ] Add watermark

## YouTube SEO (Getting Views)

### Title Optimization
**Formula:** [Benefit/How-To] + [Search Term] + [Curiosity Gap]

✅ Good titles:
- "How to take lecture notes in 50% less time"
- "AI study app review: Is it worth it?"
- "Recording biology lectures with Koala (tutorial)"

❌ Bad titles:
- "Koala.ai demo"
- "Study app"
- "New video"

### Description (500+ words)
```
[Intro - 50 words]
Learn how to [benefit] using [method].
In this video, I show you [specific outcome].

[Timestamps - 2 min]
0:00 - Intro
0:15 - Setup
2:30 - Main tutorial
5:00 - Tips
6:45 - Outro

[Main description - 300 words]
[Explain what video covers]
[Why it matters]
[Tools used]
[Related topics]

[Links - 50 words]
Download Koala: koala.ai
Follow me on Twitter: @yourhandle
Join our Discord: [link]

[Keywords/Tags at bottom]
#StudyTips #LectureNotes #StudentLife
```

### Keywords to Target

**High Intent (people looking for Koala):**
- "lecture recording app"
- "AI note taking app"
- "best study app"
- "note taking app for college"

**Medium Intent (broad study):**
- "how to take lecture notes"
- "study tips for college"
- "better notes"
- "study techniques"

**Low Intent (traffic driver):**
- "study hacks"
- "how to study"
- "college tips"
- "student life"

### Tags (Add in Upload)
```
study, college, notes, lecture, ai, app,
tutorial, study tips, how to,
koala, note taking, recording,
student, learning, education
```

### Thumbnail Design
- **Size:** 1280x720px
- **Content:**
  - Bold text (2-3 words max)
  - Your face or app screenshot
  - Bright colors (contrasting)
  - Arrows/highlights pointing to key element
  - Less text = better

**Tools:** Canva (free templates) or Photoshop

**Example thumbnail:**
- App screenshot on left
